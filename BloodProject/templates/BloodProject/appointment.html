key points about the process

Registration

You'll start by providing some basic information and ID. This takes just a few minutes

Medical Screening

A quick check of your blood pressure, pulse, and temperature. Answer a few health-related questions.

Donation

Relax in a chair. A sterile needle collects blood, usually around 10-15 minutes.

Post-Donation

Rest for a few minutes. Enjoy some refreshments.
